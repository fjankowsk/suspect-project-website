SUSPECT Paper I supplementary data published

Date Mar 17 février 2026 Tags publication / paper / data / pulsars

I just published some supplementary data for SUSPECT Paper I on Zenodo.

This includes the integrated pulse profile of PSR B1822-09 at 650 MHz obtained from our uGMRT observations on 2023-04-23, as well as the entire dataset (8723 files) of single pulse dynamic spectrum plots in PDF format. This amounts to about 5 GB of data, which is only a small fraction of the entire single-pulse dataset.

Here is the Zenodo DOI for the SUSPECT Paper I dataset.

I also submitted the pulse profile to the EPN database of pulsar profiles, where it should appear shortly.

Enjoy.
