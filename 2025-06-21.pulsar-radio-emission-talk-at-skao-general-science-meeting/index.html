Pulsar radio emission talk at SKAO General Science Meeting

Date Sam 21 juin 2025 Tags conference / travel / pulsars

I (Fabian Jankowski) recently presented our work on the Sub-GHz Radio Pulsar Emission - Lessons Learned and Predictions for SKA at the SKAO General Science Meeting in Görlitz, Germany. This included some of our NenuFAR Pulsar Census results and some work done as part of the SUSPECT project.
