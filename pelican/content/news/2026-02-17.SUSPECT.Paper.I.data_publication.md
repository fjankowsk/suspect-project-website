Title: SUSPECT Paper I supplementary data published
Date: 2026-02-17
Tags: publication, paper, data, pulsars

I just published some supplementary data for [SUSPECT Paper I](https://www.aanda.org/articles/aa/full_html/2025/03/aa51384-24/aa51384-24.html) on Zenodo.

This includes the integrated pulse profile of PSR B1822-09 at 650 MHz obtained from our uGMRT observations on 2023-04-23, as well as the entire dataset (8723 files) of single pulse dynamic spectrum plots in PDF format. This amounts to about 5 GB of data, which is only a small fraction of the entire single-pulse dataset.

Here is the Zenodo DOI for the [SUSPECT Paper I dataset](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18497372).

I also submitted the pulse profile to the [EPN database of pulsar profiles](https://psrweb.jb.man.ac.uk/epndb/), where it should appear shortly.

Enjoy.
